About Dr. Suwaibah Muhammad Nasir

Who is Dr. Suwaibah?

Dr. Suwaibah Muhammad Nasir is a solo runner from Malaysia, previously a member of the Royal Malaysian Air Force (TUDM).

She became the first Malaysian woman to run solo across the Sahara Desert, covering 1,111 km from Aswan to Port Said in Egypt over 32 days.

Record Holder

Dr. Suwaibah holds multiple records in the Malaysia Book of Records for her extraordinary achievements in endurance running and tower climbing.

Vision

To promote the sport of stairs scaling (vertical running) among Malaysian youth and establish a training academy to nurture young talent for international competitions.

Her Journey

Dr. Suwaibah Muhammad Nasir has dedicated her life to pushing the boundaries of human endurance and inspiring others to achieve their dreams. Her journey began in the Royal Malaysian Air Force (TUDM), where she developed the discipline and determination that would define her athletic career.

Her most notable achievement came when she became the first Malaysian woman to complete a solo run across the Sahara Desert, traversing 1,111 kilometers from Aswan to Port Said in Egypt over 32 grueling days. This remarkable feat demonstrated not just physical strength, but mental fortitude and unwavering commitment to her goals.

Beyond her personal achievements, Dr. Suwaibah is passionate about developing the sport of vertical running in Malaysia. She envisions establishing a world-class training academy that will nurture young Malaysian athletes and prepare them for international competitions, including the Southeast Asian Games, Commonwealth Games, and potentially the Olympic Games.
