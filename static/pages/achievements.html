Key Achievements

Sahara Desert Solo Run: 1,111 km from Aswan to Port Said, Egypt in 32 days
Avala Tower, Serbia: Successfully conquered (250m) on August 31, 2016
Baiyoke Sky Tower, Thailand: Climbed 309m tower in record time of 14 min 17 sec
Malaysia Book of Records: Multiple records holder
Royal Patronage: MM1030 mission officiated by Tengku Hassanal Ibrahim Alam Shah (Pemangku Raja Pahang)
International Recognition: Putting Malaysia on the map in vertical tower running sport

Impact & Legacy

Sports Development

The MM1030 mission is pioneering the development of vertical running as a competitive sport in Malaysia, creating pathways for young athletes to compete internationally.

Youth Inspiration

Through establishing a training academy, the mission aims to inspire and equip Malaysian youth with the skills needed to excel in vertical running competitions worldwide.

International Relations

Each tower climb carries a humanitarian message of peace, promoting international goodwill and showcasing Malaysia's commitment to unity through sport.

National Pride

Dr. Suwaibah's achievements place Malaysia prominently on the global map, demonstrating the nation's capacity to produce world-class athletes.
