The MM1030 Mission

Mission Goals

Promote peace through sport, inspire Malaysian youth, establish training academy, and prepare athletes for international competitions including SEA Games and Commonwealth Games.

Royal Patronage

The MM1030 mission was officiated by Tengku Hassanal Ibrahim Alam Shah (Pemangku Raja Pahang), who serves as the Royal Patron (Penaung Diraja) of MM1030. This royal endorsement underscores the significance of the mission in promoting Malaysia's presence on the global stage through sport and fostering international goodwill.
